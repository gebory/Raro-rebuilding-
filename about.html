WHY RARO EXISTS
Modern fashion moves quickly.

Ideas disappear before they are understood.

RARO exists as an archive where meaning comes before aesthetics, and where every mark exists for a reason.
THE MARK
The RARO Mark draws inspiration from the ancient Mesopotamian rosette.

A symbol found throughout the civilizations of Mesopotamia as a sign of continuity, identity and cultural memory.

Rather than reproducing the historical form directly, the mark was refined into a contemporary symbol.

Not a recreation.
A continuation.

The symbol represents the archive itself.
